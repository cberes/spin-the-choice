Peggy Hill

Somes games let you spin a wheel. Some games give you a choice. You take that choice, and you spin it. The name of the game is Spin the Choice!

The game is pretty straightforward. You can choose to spin or you can choose to choose. If you choose to spin, you can land on:

  • Spin
  • Choice
  • Lose a spin
  • Lose a choice
  • Free spin
  • Free choice
  • Spin again
  • Prize wheel

These are the possible outcomes on the game wheel.

There is also a prize wheel. You can spin the prize wheel when you land on Prize wheel on the game wheel. The prize wheel contains these outcomes:

  • Free spin
  • Winnebago
  • Free choice
  • Washer dryer
  • Mazda
  • Microwave oven
  • Point bank
  • Mercedes

That's about everything that we know of Spin the Choice according to Peggy Hill. (We also know you can lose.) We have had to make up some rules to fill in the holes. If you disagree with a rule or you have a suggestion, open an issue on GitHub.

Objective

The objective is to score as many points as possible. You begin the game with 3 choices and 3 spins. The game ends when, at the conclusion of a turn, you have no spins remaining and choices remaining.

Turn

At the beginning of a turn, you make a choice: to spin or to choose. You may choose to spin only if you have at least 1 spin remaining. You may choose to choose only if you have at least 1 choice remaining.

Following your choice, you must spin the game wheel. If you chose to spin, then deduct 1 spin from your spins remaining. If you chose to choose, then deduct 1 choice from your choices remaining. At this point, the turn has ended unless you landed on Prize wheel. If you landed on Prize wheel, then you must spin the prize wheel. After spinning the prize wheel, your turn has ended.

Game wheel

You must spin the game wheel after making your choice. Each outcome on the game wheel has unique consequences.

Spin

If you land on Spin after choosing Spin, then you earn 100 points. If you land on Spin after choosing Choice, then you lose 100 points.

Choice

If you land on Choice after choosing Choice, then you earn 100 points. If you land on Choice after choosing Spin, then you lose 100 points.

Lose a spin

You lose 1 spin. You cannot have fewer than zero spins remaining.

Lose a choice

You lose 1 choice. You cannot have fewer than zero choices remaining.

Free spin

You gain 1 spin.

Free choice

You gain 1 choice.

Spin again

If you chose Choice, then you gain 1 choice. If you chose Spin, then you gain 1 spin. You must use this spin or choice immediately.

Prize wheel

You must spin the prize wheel. A cow bell sounds.

Prize wheel

You must spin the prize wheel after landing on Prize wheel on the game wheel. There are eight possible outcomes. Most of the outcomes award you a prize. (Note: no outcome awards you a real prize.) These such prizes have a corresponding value in points. The exceptions to this type of prize are described below.

Free spin

You gain 1 spin.

Free choice

You gain 1 choice.

Point bank

The amount of points in the point bank is added to your point total. Then, the point bank is reset to zero. After every spin of the game wheel, 10 points are added to the bank. The point bank does not reset at the end of the game. (But the bank will reset if you close your browser or refresh the page.)
